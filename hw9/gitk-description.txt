Subwindows of gitk

upperleft: it is a hierarchical tree of all the commits with different branches
and relationships.

The node at first is HEAD, then what follows is local branch and then that node connects to
remote branch master on origin, then the end of the line is commit message.

uppermiddle: it is the contact information of the authors of the commit. 

upperright: it is the time that the patch is committed. 

middleL there is a bar of SHA of selected commit.

lowerleft: it is the ChangeLog of the commit, and also comes with tools like search or diff

lowerright: it is a list of files that are impacted by this commit
